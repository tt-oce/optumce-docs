# 1. Shallow foundation
This example deals with the an eccentrically loaded foundation. The soil is
saturated clay and the analysis is to be performed assuming undrained conditions. For this purpose a total stress analysis approach is adopted. The soil is modeled by means of the Tresca model with an undrained shear strength $s_u = 30$ kPa and an undrained Youngâ€™s modulus of $E_u = 40$ MPa. The foundation is modeled as Rigid material with a unit weight of 24kN/m3.
